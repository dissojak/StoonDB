# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private abuse

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, or issues that do
not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issue trackers,
pull requests, and community discussions.
